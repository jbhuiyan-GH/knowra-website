KNOWRA WEBSITE — EDITORIAL REVIEW PACKAGE
=========================================

What this is
------------
A static HTML mockup of the KNOWRA website. It is for content and editorial
review, not the production build. Read the copy, check the tone, flag anything
that reads wrong for a Bangladeshi B2B audience, and note anything inaccurate.

How to open it
--------------
Open index.html in a web browser. Keep an internet connection on, because the
fonts (Hanken Grotesk and Newsreader) load from Google Fonts. Without internet
the text still reads fine, it just uses a fallback font.

Click the KNOWRA wordmark or the top navigation to move between pages. The
footer links, the "Talk to a person" buttons, and the floating pill all work.

Pages
-----
index.html ................ Home
knowra_capabilities.html .. What we build (4 capabilities)
knowra_showroom.html ...... Worked examples (4 exhibits)
knowra_industries.html .... Four sectors we focus on
knowra_resources.html ..... Free guide, writing, case studies
knowra_about.html ......... Why we exist, beliefs, the team
knowra_contact.html ....... Contact
knowra_honest_guide.html .. The free guide itself (the lead magnet)

The brand voice to check against
--------------------------------
Honest, plain, no hype. Short sentences. American English. The locked brand
line is: "Judgment stays with people and the speed comes from AI." We never
oversell AI and we always name its limits.

What is still placeholder (do not treat as final)
--------------------------------------------------
- Logo: the KNOWRA wordmark stands in for a logo for now.
- Team photos: the three people on the About page need photos.
- Contact form and the guide email box: these are mockups. They do not send
  anything yet. A real form needs a backend when you build it.
- Footer links "Writing" and "Case studies": no pages yet, they point nowhere.
- The four article titles on the Resources page are a content plan, not
  published pieces. Do not link them live until they exist.

What is accurate and checked
----------------------------
The PwC figures and the 2025 data-law summary in the guide were verified
against current sources. If you change those numbers, re-check them first.

Notes for the build
--------------------
This is a single-column, mobile-first layout at 660px max width. Theme is warm
monochrome on cream with one burnt-orange accent (#bc5a2b). Type is heavy
Hanken Grotesk headlines over Newsreader serif body. Keep these in the rebuild.

Send edits back as comments or a marked-up copy, whatever is easiest for you.
